Privacy Statement

Your privacy is important to us. Everything we do at Soch: Mindful Growth is driven by our core values of respect, transparency, and trust.

Our comprehensive privacy policy, details our practices. Here's a summary of key points:

  • We may process personal information based on your interactions with our Services.
  • We do not process sensitive personal information.
  • We do not receive information from third parties.
  • Your information is processed to provide, improve, and administer our Services, communicate with you, ensure security, prevent fraud, and comply with laws.
  • We only share information in specific situations and with specific third parties.
  • We implement security measures to protect your data, but no method is 100% secure.
  • You have rights regarding your personal information, which may vary based on your location.

We're not in the business of selling your data. We never have been and never will be. Additionally, we do not use your data for AI training or any purposes beyond those explicitly stated in our privacy policy.

To exercise your rights or for any questions, please contact us at help@maxentlabs.com.

Our commitment is to provide you with a secure, privacy-focused environment for your personal growth journey.
